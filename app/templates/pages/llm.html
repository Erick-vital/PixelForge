LLM manager

Centralized provider integration

Use LlmGenerationService.generate_text(...) from domain services. Routes should not know provider details or API-key lookup rules.

await LlmGenerationService().generate_text(
    system_prompt="You are concise.",
    prompt=input_text,
    provider=provider,
    model=model,
    base_url=base_url,
    temperature=0.2,
    max_tokens=2000,
)

Secrets are read server-side from APP_LLM_API_KEY, APP_OPENAI_API_KEY, or APP_ANTHROPIC_API_KEY and are never logged.
